RoofMaxx: The $5,200 Alternative to a $40,000 Replacement

What Is RoofMaxx?

RoofMaxx is a roof restoration treatment that does something no traditional roofer can do: it actually restores your shingles instead of tearing them off.

How It Works

Asphalt shingles contain natural oils that keep them flexible and waterproof. Over time, these oils dry out — causing shingles to become brittle, crack, and lose their protective granules.

RoofMaxx is a plant-based treatment that penetrates your shingles and replenishes those lost oils. It uses soy methyl ester technology — the same chemistry found in high-performance Goodyear tires, adapted for roofing by scientists from Battelle Memorial Institute.

The treatment is sprayed directly onto your existing roof. It absorbs in about 30 minutes. The entire process takes roughly two hours — no mess, no tear-off, no crew in your life for a week.

The Result

Your shingles regain their flexibility and waterproofing ability. Your roof is protected for another 5 years per treatment. With up to three treatments, you add up to 15 years to your roof's life.
