Why Central Valley Roofs Need Special Attention

Extreme Summer Heat

Fresno summers regularly exceed 105°F. Prolonged UV exposure and thermal cycling accelerate oil loss in asphalt shingles — the exact problem RoofMaxx was designed to reverse.

Valley Winter Rains

When the rains come, compromised shingles let water in fast. A roof that looks fine in August becomes a liability by January. Early intervention with RoofMaxx prevents this cycle.

Aging Housing Stock

Many Central Valley homes were built in the 1970s–1990s and are approaching end-of-original-roof life. Most are prime RoofMaxx candidates — and most homeowners don't know it yet.
