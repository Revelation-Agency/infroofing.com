What We Actually Check

Our OSHA-trained inspector goes through your entire roofing system — top to bottom. Here's exactly what's covered in every free inspection.

✓
Shingle Condition

We look for cracking, curling, cupping, blistering, and missing shingles — the primary signs your roof is losing integrity from UV and heat exposure.

✓
Granule Loss Assessment

Granule loss is how shingles age. We check for bare spots, granule buildup in gutters, and overall surface coverage — key indicators for RoofMaxx eligibility.

✓
Flashing & Seals

Flashing around chimneys, vents, skylights, and valleys is where most leaks start. We check every seal and transition point for gaps, rust, or lifting.

✓
Gutter & Drainage

Clogged or damaged gutters push water back under your roofline. We check gutter condition, slope, and drainage to catch problems before they cause interior damage.

✓
Vents & Ventilation

Attic ventilation directly affects how fast your shingles age — especially in Fresno's summer heat. We assess ridge vents, soffit vents, and overall airflow.

✓
Moisture & Attic Signs

We check for signs of moisture intrusion, water staining, and deck damage that indicate an active or past leak — problems that get much worse if left unaddressed.
