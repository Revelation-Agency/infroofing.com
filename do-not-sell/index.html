Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

For California residents, under the CCPA/CPRA

Your Right to Opt Out

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of their personal information. Infinite Roofing Solutions does not sell your personal information for money. However, we do use third-party advertising and analytics tools (such as the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics) that, under California law, may be considered "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

You can opt out at any time using one of the methods below. Your choice will be honored on this website and reflected in our records.

How to Opt Out

You have three options:

1. Use Our Cookie Consent Banner

If you have not already made a choice, the cookie banner at the bottom of any page lets you Decline non-essential cookies. Selecting Decline disables our advertising and analytics tools (Meta Pixel, Google Analytics) on your device for this website.

If you have previously accepted and want to change your choice, you can clear your decision by opening your browser's developer console on infroofing.com and running:

localStorage.removeItem('consent.v1'); location.reload();

The banner will reappear and you can choose Decline.

2. Send Us a Verifiable Opt-Out Request

Email us at privacy@infroofing.com with the subject line "Do Not Sell or Share — California Resident". Please include:

  • Your full name
  • The phone number and/or email address you have used with us
  • Confirmation that you are a California resident

If you don't have email, you can call us at (559) 215-8724 and ask for the Privacy Request line. We will confirm your identity using information you have already given us so that we don't act on a request from someone other than you.

3. Use a Browser-Level Opt-Out Signal

We respect the Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signal where it is technically detectable. If your browser sends a GPC signal, we will treat that as a request to opt out for that browser session.

What Happens After You Opt Out

We will:

  • Stop loading the Meta Pixel, Meta Conversions API, and Google Analytics on this site for your device
  • Not share new personal information with those third parties for advertising purposes
  • Honor your opt-out for at least 12 months before asking again, as required by California law

Opting out will not stop you from being able to request a free inspection, call us, or use the website normally. We will still need basic contact information to contact you about a service request.

Authorized Agents

You may use an authorized agent to submit an opt-out request on your behalf. We will need written permission from you authorizing the agent, and we may verify your identity directly.

No Discrimination

We will not deny service, charge a different price, or provide a lower quality of service because you exercised your privacy rights.

Other California Rights

In addition to opting out, California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, request that we delete it, and request that we correct it. Those rights are described in our Privacy Policy.
