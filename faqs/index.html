RoofMaxx & Roof Restoration

RoofMaxx is a bio-based oil treatment developed by scientists from Battelle Memorial Institute — the same institute that developed Febreze for Procter & Gamble. As asphalt shingles age, they lose their natural oils and become brittle. RoofMaxx replaces those oils at the molecular level, restoring flexibility and extending the life of the shingles by up to 5 years per treatment. The formula is EPA-registered, USDA BioPreferred, and backed by independent lab testing.

RoofMaxx works on asphalt shingles that still have their structural integrity — no missing shingles, no exposed deck boards, no active leaks through the structure. The treatment restores flexibility; it can't fix shingles that have already crumbled or failed. We assess this during your free inspection. Roughly 85% of roofs we inspect qualify. If yours doesn't, we'll tell you upfront.

A RoofMaxx treatment for a typical Fresno home costs around $5,200. A full asphalt shingle replacement runs $35,000–$45,000 for the same home. If your roof qualifies for treatment — and most do — you're looking at saving $30,000–$40,000 while getting 5 more years of life per treatment, repeatable up to three times. The math is straightforward.
