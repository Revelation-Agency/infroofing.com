When Re-Roofing Is the Right Call

Most Fresno homeowners quoted for replacement don't actually need a new roof — but some do. Here are the honest signs replacement is the real answer.

Your Roof Is 25+ Years Old

Asphalt shingles have a real-world lifespan of 20–30 years in the Central Valley. Past that, even RoofMaxx can't reverse the damage — the shingle mat itself has failed.

Deck Damage or Active Rot

Sagging rooflines, soft spots when walked on, or visible rot in the decking mean the structural wood beneath your shingles has failed. That's a replacement job, not a restoration.

Widespread Shingle Failure

If more than 30% of shingles are cracked, curling, or missing granules, repair and restoration stop making financial sense. A full replacement gives you 25+ years of fresh protection.

Multiple Active Leaks

One leak is a repair. Three or four active leaks in different places usually means the underlayment and flashing system have failed system-wide — and replacement is more cost-effective than chasing leaks.

Storm or Hail Damage (Insurance Claim)

Catastrophic storm events — sustained high winds, large hail, fallen trees — often require a full replacement through your insurance carrier. We work directly with adjusters and document everything properly.

Selling or Buying a Home

Real estate transactions often require roof certifications. If yours won't pass certification and the remaining life is too short for buyer lending, replacement is the cleanest path to close.

Not sure which category you're in? Start with a free inspection — we'll give you an honest read.

Our Re-Roofing Process

A full replacement is a big project. Here's exactly what to expect from the first inspection to the final magnetic sweep.

1

Inspection & Quote

We inspect the full roof, decking, ventilation, and flashing system. You get a detailed written estimate with material options, timeline, and transparent pricing.

2

Tear-Off & Deck Inspection

Complete removal of the old roof down to decking. We inspect every board, repair rotted or damaged decking, and confirm proper attic ventilation before moving on.

3

Install to Spec

Premium synthetic underlayment, ice & water shield where required, new flashing at every penetration, and your chosen roofing material installed to manufacturer spec.

4

Cleanup & Warranty

Full magnetic sweep for nails, complete property cleanup, and final walk-through. You receive both the manufacturer warranty and our workmanship warranty in writing.

Most residential re-roofs complete in 1–3 days depending on size and material.
