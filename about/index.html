The Roofer Who Can't Lie to You

Most roofing contractors make their money one way: recommending $40,000 replacements. They have no financial incentive to tell you your roof is fine. So they don't.

Infinite Roofing Solutions is different in one very specific way: we don't do replacements.

"We can't profit from telling you that you need a new roof. Our only service is the $5,200 RoofMaxx restoration — and we'll only recommend it if it's actually right for your roof."

That means when we inspect your roof, we have exactly one incentive: to tell you the truth. If your roof qualifies for restoration, we'll explain exactly how and why. If it doesn't — if it truly needs replacement — we'll tell you that, and recommend you find a reputable replacement contractor.

We won't see a dime either way if replacement is the honest answer. That's the only reason you can trust us.
