Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

Infinite Roofing Solutions operates infroofing.com. We are a licensed roofing contractor based in Fresno, California (CSLB #1126804). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or request our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a free inspection request form
  • Call our phone number
  • Email us directly

This information may include your name, phone number, email address, home address, and any details you share about your roof. We do not collect payment information through our website.

We also collect certain technical information automatically, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time on site. This is collected through Google Analytics and Meta Pixel (Facebook) tracking technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you to schedule your free roof inspection
  • Answer questions about our services
  • Follow up after your inspection if you request it

We use technical data (via Google Analytics and Meta Pixel) to understand how visitors use our website and to show relevant ads to people who may be interested in our services.

Advertising & Tracking Technologies

Meta Pixel: We use the Meta Pixel (Facebook Pixel) on our website. This technology allows us to measure the effectiveness of our advertising by understanding the actions people take on our website. The Meta Pixel collects data about your activity on our site, which may be used to show you relevant ads on Facebook and Instagram. Data is processed by Meta Platforms, Inc. under their Privacy Policy.

Meta Conversions API: We use Meta's Conversions API to send certain event data (page views, form submissions, phone call clicks) server-side to Meta for ad measurement purposes. This data is processed with privacy-preserving hashing where applicable.

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and visitor behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects anonymized data about how you use our site. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (such as our scheduling and CRM tools), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Meta Platforms and Google, as described in the advertising section above
  • Law enforcement or government agencies if required by law

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. If you would like your data deleted, please contact us at the information below.

Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at the information below.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and to support our advertising and analytics. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You can adjust cookie settings through your browser, but doing so may affect certain features of the site.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
