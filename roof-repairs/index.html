What We Repair

We handle the most common residential roof repair issues in Fresno and the Central Valley. Every repair comes with an honest assessment of your roof's overall condition — no spin.

Roof Leaks

Active leaks diagnosed and repaired at the source — not just patched at the surface. We trace where water is actually entering, not just where it's showing up inside.

Storm Damage Repair

Wind, hail, and fallen branches leave specific damage patterns. We assess what's actually damaged and repair only what needs it — no unnecessary upselling.

Flashing Repair

Chimney flashing, pipe boot seals, valley flashing, and skylight seals — among the most common leak sources and among the most overlooked. We get them right.

Missing or Damaged Shingles

Individual shingle replacement when localized damage doesn't require a full-roof action. We match your existing shingles as closely as possible and seal properly.

Gutter Repair & Cleaning

Clogged or damaged gutters cause water to back up under your shingles. We clean, reseat, and repair gutters as part of a complete roof health approach.

Fascia & Soffit Repair

Rotted or damaged fascia boards allow water and pests into your eaves. Soffit damage compromises attic ventilation. We repair both before they become bigger problems.

Flat Roof Repair

Ponding water, blistering, and membrane failures on flat or low-slope roofs require a different approach. We diagnose and repair flat roofing systems correctly.

Honest Condition Assessment

Every repair visit includes a straight read on your roof's overall condition. If RoofMaxx could extend your roof's life, we'll say so. If replacement is the real answer, we'll say that too.
