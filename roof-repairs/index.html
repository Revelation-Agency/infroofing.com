What We Repair

We handle the most common residential roof repair issues in Fresno and the Central Valley. Every repair comes with an honest assessment of your roof's overall condition — no spin.

Roof Leaks

Active leaks diagnosed and repaired at the source — not just patched at the surface. We find where water is actually entering.

Storm Damage

Wind, hail, and fallen branches leave specific damage patterns. We assess what's actually affected and repair only what needs it.

Flashing Repair

Chimney flashing, pipe boot seals, valley flashing — these are among the most common leak sources and among the most overlooked.

Missing or Damaged Shingles

Individual shingle replacement when localized damage doesn't require full-roof action. We match your existing shingles as closely as possible.

Gutter Repair & Cleaning

Clogged or damaged gutters cause water to back up under your shingles. We clean, reseat, and repair gutters as part of a complete roof health approach.

Honest Condition Assessment

Every repair visit includes an honest read on your roof's overall condition. If RoofMaxx could extend your roof's life significantly, we'll say so. If replacement is the real answer, we'll say that too.
