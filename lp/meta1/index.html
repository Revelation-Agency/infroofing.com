What Is RoofMaxx?

RoofMaxx is a roof restoration treatment that does something no traditional roofer can do: it actually restores your shingles instead of tearing them off.

How It Works

Asphalt shingles contain natural oils that keep them flexible and waterproof. Over time, these oils dry out, causing shingles to become brittle, crack, and lose their protective granules.

RoofMaxx is a plant-based treatment that penetrates your shingles and replenishes those lost oils. It’s the same technology used in award-winning Goodyear tires, adapted for roofing.

The treatment is sprayed directly onto your existing roof. It absorbs in about 30 minutes. The entire process takes roughly two hours, with no mess, no tear-off, and no crew tramping through your home for a week.

The Result

Your shingles regain their flexibility and waterproofing ability. Your roof is protected for another 5 years per treatment. With up to three treatments, you can add up to 15 years to your roof’s life.
